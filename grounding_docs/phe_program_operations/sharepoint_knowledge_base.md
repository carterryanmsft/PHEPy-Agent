## Overview
The CxE Security Care SharePoint sites are the primary knowledge repositories for the Customer Experience (CxE) Security Care Customer Engineering Manager (CEM) team, with specialized focus on Purview and Cloud Encryption Services (CES) products. These sites contain operational documentation, team contacts, processes, guidelines, and historical records essential for PHEPy orchestrator operations.

### Resource Categories

#### Core Operations
- **CEM Team Site**: General CEM operations, cross-product processes, and team-wide resources
- **CxE CARE Home**: Quick access link to main CARE site (aka.ms shortcut)
- **Key Resources**: Central directory of essential links, tools, documentation, and daily references

#### Onboarding & Training
- **CARE General Onboarding**: 4-module program covering culture, structure, and tools
- **Overview of CxE CARE**: 6.5-hour foundational module on mission, vision, and org structure
- **CEM Purview New Hire Onboarding**: Product-specific setup, SAW configuration, and training
- **Meet the Team**: Detailed team roster with roles and contact information

#### Purview & Product Operations
- **Purview Hub**: Dashboards, CRI workflows, team lists, and operational links
- **Process Workflows**: Playbooks for CRIs, GetHelp, MCS, escalations, and investigations
- **Customer Escalation Management**: Overview of CEM verticals (Entra, Intune, MDE, MDO, etc.)

#### Intensive Care (IC) Program
- **IC Customer Guidelines**: Procedures and protocols for IC customers
- **IC Program Management**: CRM workflows, alumni onboarding, engagement extensions
- **IC/MCS Onboarding Form**: Dashboard inclusion/removal requests

#### Dashboards & Reporting
- **Purview Care Dashboard**: Cases, DCRs, bugs, escalations, and engagement metrics
- **Shared Services BI**: Cross-team metrics and reporting
- **DSP Escalations Dashboard**: Monthly escalation tracking and analysis

#### Incident & Access Management
- **IcM Portal**: Incident management and tracking
- **IcM Escalation Templates**: Submission procedures (e.g., Entra ID)
- **Auto-JIT Systems**: Just-in-time access for DRI and classified customers

#### Engineering & Escalations
- **Endpoint DLP Process**: MIP team escalation procedures
- **Engineering Wikis**: Product-specific escalation paths and troubleshooting

#### Mission Critical Services
- **MCS Overview**: Mission Critical Services offerings and structure
- **Security 360**: Comprehensive customer security posture view

## Integration with PHEPy Workspace

### Local Files vs SharePoint
- **Local `grounding_docs/`**: Contains exported/cached versions of critical SharePoint content for offline access and faster agent retrieval
- **SharePoint (live)**: Always has the most current information and should be consulted for real-time updates

### Recommended Workflow
1. **For static information**: Use local grounding docs (faster, cached)
2. **For dynamic information**: Reference SharePoint (team changes, active incidents, current priorities)
3. **For verification**: Cross-check local docs against SharePoint if data seems outdated

## Access & Permissions
- **Authentication**: Microsoft corporate credentials required
- **Permissions**: Team members have Read access by default; Contribute access for content owners
- **External Access**: Not available (internal Microsoft only)

## Update Frequency
- **Team roster**: Updated as changes occur
- **Meeting notes**: Weekly (after WSR/MBR meetings)
- **Procedures**: Quarterly or as process changes
- **Customer assignments**: Monthly or as accounts shift
