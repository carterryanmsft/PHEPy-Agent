
Value Stream Kaizen is a structured, time‑bound improvement workshop (2–4 days) designed to analyze a full process, identify inefficiencies, and rapidly implement improvements. It brings cross‑functional teams together to deeply understand current workflows and redesign them for better performance.

Purpose:
- Deepen understanding of the current state.
- Accelerate improvement outcomes and empower teams.
- Optimize entire processes with multiple departments or waste.

Participants:
- CI Facilitator
- Sponsor/Leader
- Process Owners
- Process Executors

Four Steps:
1. Understand Current State — map the workflow.
2. Identify Pain Points — surface waste and non‑value‑add steps.
3. Identify Solutions — brainstorm and prioritize improvements.
4. Create Execution Plan — define owners, timelines, and KPI sustainment.
