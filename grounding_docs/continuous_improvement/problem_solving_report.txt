
The Problem Solving Report (PSR) is a structured communication tool to align teams, clarify gaps, and accelerate impact. Used when misalignment exists, acceleration is needed, or support is required.

Roles:
- KPI DRI
- KPI Team Members
- Executive Sponsor

Core PSR structure:
- Where Are We?
- What Do We Need?
- What Has Been Done?
- What’s Next?

DIVE Framework:
Define → Investigate → Validate → Execute.

PSR Components:
- Clarify the Gap
- Trend the Gap
- Identify Gap Concentration
- Identify Root Cause
- Generate Countermeasures
- Experiment to Derisk
- Execute with Rigor
